Library

The college library is a knowledge hub containing thousands of books, journals, magazines, and digital resources. Students can access academic textbooks, research papers, novels, and reference materials. A peaceful reading space with individual desks and reading lamps is provided. Digital library access offers e-books and online journals 24x7. Wi-Fi and computers are available for research and study. The library is managed by experienced librarians. Book issuing, return, and renewal processes are completely automated. Open-shelf system allows easy access to books. Students can also suggest books to be added. Group study areas are also provided.

Future Technologies

We train our students in future technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science, and Cloud Computing. Regular seminars and workshops are conducted on trending technologies. Our labs support practicals in IoT, Robotics, and AR/VR. Special interest groups (SIGs) are created for students passionate about emerging tech. Industry mentors are assigned to guide final year research. Coding clubs and AI labs promote curiosity and future-ready skills. Collaboration with tech startups ensures live exposure. We prepare students to be industry-ready for tomorrow.

Real World Experience

Our curriculum focuses on real-world learning through projects, internships, and industry collaborations. Students are encouraged to build live applications and solve real-life problems. Guest lectures by industry experts provide valuable insights into current trends. Industrial visits and corporate tie-ups help students understand practical workflows. Final year projects are reviewed by professionals from reputed companies. Participation in coding competitions, hackathons, and innovation challenges is encouraged. Our alumni also guide students by sharing their experiences. Students get a chance to work with NGOs, startups, and MNCs. Resume-building and interview sessions are organized regularly. Computer Lab

Our college offers state-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and high-speed internet. Each system is loaded with essential software required for programming, design, and development. The lab is fully air-conditioned and managed by experienced lab assistants. Students get hands-on experience in coding, networking, and project development. Regular workshops on Python, Java, Web Development, and Cyber Security are conducted here. The lab supports practical sessions for BCA, MCA, and IT students. Labs remain open during extended hours during project season. CCTV surveillance ensures a safe and secure learning environment. Students also get exposure to real-time debugging and software deployment. The lab promotes independent and group-based learning.

Sports Facilities

Our college promotes physical fitness and team spirit through excellent sports infrastructure. We have playgrounds for cricket, football, and volleyball, and indoor facilities for badminton, table tennis, and chess. Students regularly participate in inter-college and state-level tournaments. A dedicated sports coach guides students and prepares them for competitions. Fitness and yoga sessions are conducted weekly. Equipment is maintained and upgraded regularly. Annual Sports Day is celebrated with enthusiasm and rewards. Girls and boys both have equal access to all facilities. We encourage students to balance academics and athletics. A special sports club is also formed for interested students.
